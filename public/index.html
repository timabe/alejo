Villa Alejo History

Welcome to Villa Alejo, an iconic building nestled in the heart of Palm Springs. Originally constructed in the 1960s, Villa Alejo stands as a testament to the mid-century modern architecture that has made Palm Springs a renowned destination for design enthusiasts. The building's unique blend of classic elegance and modern amenities provides a perfect backdrop for a relaxing getaway. Each unit boasts spacious layouts, thoughtfully designed interiors, and large windows that frame stunning views of the surrounding desert landscape. Featured in Palm Spring's modernism week..
